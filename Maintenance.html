Why should I do maintenance?

Swimming pools must be under constant supervision for many reasons, one of these is the fact that water is the main incubator and transmitter of bacteria and parasites.

These affect the aesthetics of the pool, turning it into a swamp water appearance and making surfaces slippery. Making it totally unpleasant to use.

Factors and Maintenance

Before starting any process, we must know the conditions of the swimming pool. For this our specialized team must carry out an inspection, targeting problems like green water, cloudy water, slippery floors, viscous wall. and others.

Disinfect

A deep cleaning of the water applying chlorine for rapid disinfection, which destroys the microorganisms in the water

Ph balance

PH is a coefficient that measures the concentration of hydrogen ions in water. PH is the factor with the greatest impact on properly balanced water. The smaller the pH unit, the more acidic the solution. Conversely, the larger the pH unit, the more alkaline the pool water will be.

Crystalline waters

It is advisable to apply an algaecide constantly. This is because the algae are the main element for the cultivation of fungi and bacteria, and causes the green coloration of it, bad smells, and slippery surfaces. Measures should be taken as soon as possible to avoid complications to the pools and the pumping system.

Flocculants

To always have an environment with 100% crystalline water, you must eliminate the particles that exist in the water such as dust, salts. Also, these could clog some filters. For this, some chemical materials are used that manage to agglutinate these particles and then be cleaned.

Cleaning

We perform a rigorous cleaning service, including services to filters, pumps, skimmers, and more.
