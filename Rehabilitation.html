Rehabilitation

If the pool acquires that "swamp" appearance due to the accumulation of algae and solid particles then an acid wash is necessary

Acid Wash

Acid washing is a process that uses chemicals like muriatic acid combined with water, Once the mixture is applied, our team of professionals will remove algae and dirt from the surface of the pool.

In addition to the aforementioned, there are other opportunities where an acid wash is ideal for swimming pools such as:

  • Magnesium stains:
  • Copper stains:
  • Hard Water:
  • Algae growth

Venezia Pools

Why call us for an acid wash?

The entire process uses aggressive chemicals, so if you are not a professional it can be very easy to injure yourself with these materials, not only you, it could also damage the structural condition of your pool, costing you more maintenance in repairs, if not performed correctly this may cause:

  • Chemical burns to the skin
  • Discoloration of surfaces
  • Lung damage
  • Fainting
