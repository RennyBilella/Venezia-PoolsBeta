About Us

Venezia pools was born from the need to provide a quality, economical and responsible service when it comes to encompassing everything that has to do with rehabilitation and maintenance for swimming pool areas and water pumping systems. We are specialists in completely restoring your pools no matter what state of deterioration it is, as well as providing them with the necessary care so that they are always in their optimal state no matter the circumstances and seasons. And all this without looking away from producing the least impact on the environment.

Mission

Respond and meet all the needs of our customers, always offering solutions that are adequate from a technical and economic point of view, without neglecting the safety of our employees and users. And all this adapting to what the client wants and needs.

Sight

Our aim is to achieve maximum customer satisfaction to the point that they give us their trust and recommendation to other users, and we will achieve all this by surrounding ourselves with the best professionals in the area and using only the best materials and top quality products.
